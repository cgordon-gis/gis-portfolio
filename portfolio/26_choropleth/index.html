Choropleth

Scrabble scores for Parishes and Non-Civil Parished Areas in Shropshire

Inspiration came from @dgplacenames on Twitter.

Data I used

  • Parishes and Non Civil Parished Areas from the Office of National Statistics

Tools I used

  • Python, particularly Pandas, to generate the scrabble scores for each parish name.
  • QGIS to create the layout.

What did I learn?

  • That there are fewer “l"s in choropleth than I thought!
  • More about tidying up data to use in pandas!

What do I want to do next?

  • Find out more about different ways to classify choropleth data and the differences it makes to the message.
