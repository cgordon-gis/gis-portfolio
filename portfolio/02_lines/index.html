Lines

My thoughts for the theme of lines turned towards routes, e.g. railways. In the end I went for a route from one of our extremely enjoyable walks in the Highlands around Torridon.

3D line with labels

Inspiration taken from 3D line for a route.

Tools I used

  • QGIS with QGIS2threejs extension

What did I learn?

  • How to add z values to lines and points
  • Setting up labels in a 3D view in QGIS2threejs and add connectors to points.

What do I want to change?

  • Find a straighter route? Maybe the bike ride from Achnasheen to Torridon? The twiddle in the middle of this one around Meall nan Caepairean and An Ruadh Stac isn’t great.
  • Fonts etc - Alastair Rae has a tutorial with other suggestions I could try.
