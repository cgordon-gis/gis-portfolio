About my portfolio

During November 2021, 30DaysOfMaps ran on Twitter. Started by Tuki Tjukanov several years ago, this involves map makers, cartographers and anyone else with an interest, creating maps for a series of 30 prompts.

I didn’t have time in November 2021 to take part but decided to spread it over the following year. This portfolio is the result of my efforts.

I set myself the following parameters:

  • Try to use entirely open data so I can share the end results online.
  • Make sure I put full acknowledgements on everything.
  • Try out some new techniques and open source software, and make careful notes of where I found instructions, or what I did.
  • Think about what I learnt from creating each map.

I still have some to go, and have multiple maps for some of the prompts. It’s been great fun and I’ve learnt a lot.
